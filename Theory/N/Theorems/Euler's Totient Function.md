$\varphi(n)$ - number of integers less than $n$ that are relatively prime to $n$.
if $m,n$ are coprime.
+ $\varphi(mn)=\varphi(n)\varphi(m)$
+ $\varphi(n) = n(1-\frac{1}{p_1})(1-\frac{1}{p_2})\cdots(1-\frac{1}{p_k})= p_1^{\alpha_1-1}(p_1{-}1)\,p_2^{\alpha_2-1}(p_2{-}1)\cdots p_k^{\alpha_k-1}(p_k{-}1)$ ,where $n = p_1^{\alpha_1} p_2^{\alpha_2} \cdots p_k^{\alpha_k}$
+ $\left(\frac{6}{\pi^2}\right)n^2<\sigma(n)\varphi(n)<n^2$ ($\sigma$ - [[Sum of Divisors]])
