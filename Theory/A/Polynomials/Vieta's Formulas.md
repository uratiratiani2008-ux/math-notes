$P(x)=a_nx^n+a_{n-1}x^{n-1}+...+a_1x+a_0$
has $n$ (not necessarily distinct) complex roots: $r_1,r_2,...r_n$. $P(x)=a_n(x-r_1)(x-r_2)...(x-r_n)$
$r_1+r_2+...+r_n=-\frac{a_{n-1}}{a_n}$
$(r_1r_2+r_1r_3+...r_1r_n)+(r_2r_3+r_2r_4+...+r_2r_n)+...+r_{n-1}r_n=\frac{a_{n-2}}{a_n}$
.
.
.
$r_1r_2...r_n=(-1)^n\frac{a_0}{a_n}$

For quadratic:
$P(x)=ax^2+bx+c$
$r_1+r_2=-\frac{b}{a}$
$r_1r_2=\frac{c}{a}$
