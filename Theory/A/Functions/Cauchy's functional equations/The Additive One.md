$f:\mathbb{Q}\rightarrow\mathbb{Q}$
$f(x+y)=f(x)+f(y) \Rightarrow f(x)=cx : c\in\mathbb{R}$
------------------------
to transfer it to $f:\mathbb{R}\rightarrow\mathbb{R}$ one of these needs to happen:

+ $f$ is monotonous on any interval.
+ $f$ is continuous. 
+ $f(x)\geq 0$ or $f(x)≤0$ $\forall x\geq 0$
+ $f$ is bounded above or below on any interval.
